Lessons Learned

  • Using different denominators can highlight different issues along the network
  • What to use is influenced by crime type, data availability, resources available for interpreting the data
  • Denominators exist along an availability-validity spectrum

Next steps

  • Focus further on demographics to identify relative risks instead of risk based on general population.
  • Incorporate method for systematic inclusion of context through environmental backcloth where relevant as well.
